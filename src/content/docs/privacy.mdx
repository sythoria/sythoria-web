# Privacy & Security

Privacy isn't a feature in Sythoria — it's the foundation. Here's exactly how your data is handled.

## API keys stay local

Your API keys are stored **exclusively in your browser's localStorage**. They are:

- ❌ Never sent to our servers
- ❌ Never included in analytics or logs
- ❌ Never shared with third parties
- ✅ Only used to authenticate directly with your chosen AI provider

## No server-side storage

Sythoria's backend is a thin proxy that forwards requests to AI provider APIs. We do not:

- Store your conversations
- Log your prompts or responses
- Retain any personal data

Conversations exist only in your browser's local state. Closing the tab or clearing storage removes them entirely.

## Zero telemetry

Sythoria does not collect any usage data, analytics, or tracking information. There are no:

- Analytics scripts
- Tracking cookies
- Error reporting services that phone home

## Local models

For maximum privacy, use Sythoria with **Ollama** or any local model server. With local models:

- No data leaves your machine
- No API key is needed
- No network connection required (after model download)

See the [Ollama setup guide](/docs/providers/ollama) to get started.
